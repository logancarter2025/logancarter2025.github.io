Union College Sports History Analysis

I created a python function to automatically read through every Union College newspapers from 1880 to 2000. From here, I count the mention of certain sports in the newspapers, and analyze and graph the lexical density of each sport. From the sentences which mention sports, I measure the average sentence-level polarity, which is used to decide whether a sentence is negative or positive. I compare my findings with the actual results of the Union sports teams during these years. Here is a link to the full project.
